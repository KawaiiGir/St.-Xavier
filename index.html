    1. Accuracy: Humans are often used to error and are sometimes unsure about accuracy of some devices. Our attendence management systems are perfectly accurate of the time when your child arrives and also notifies you if your child did not arrives on time.

It saves money by putting an end to innacurate time reporting, buddy punching, absentism, tardiness, time abuse, and overpayment. As a matter of fact, a study done by a list-group found that large organizations save up to $1,600 per employee after implementing automated attendence management systems.
